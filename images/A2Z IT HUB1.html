Classroom training

Classroom training is an instructional method where the course content and learning material are taught in-person to a group of participants by the domain expert. This allows a scope for a face – to – face interaction between the participants and the instructor. Moreover, participants benefit from peer learning as they also engage with fellow participants. TCB is capable of conducting classroom training on a single topic spanning over 1 to 5 days or hold training bundles. This will allow participants to attain additional guidance from the experts directly.
